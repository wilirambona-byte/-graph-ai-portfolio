Vision

Bridging Public Health and Intelligence

I am a Medical Doctor currently completing an MSc in Public Health and Health Promotion at Bangor University, UK. I have over four years of experience working with Ministries of Health, NGOs, and international partners to strengthen community health systems across low-resource and diverse settings, including Burundi, the Democratic Republic of Congo, and Uganda.

My work focuses on optimising resources, strengthening Community Health Worker (CHW) programmes, and supporting data-driven programme design and implementation. As interest in digital and AI-enabled health solutions grows, I am particularly interested in the practical, ethical, and evidence-based use of digital and generative technologies to support health system strengthening.
